Admission

  • Admission for the academic session begins in the month of January.
  • Completion of 3 years 6 months on 31st July is a must for Jr. KG class admission. For Sr.KG, the child should be 4 years and 6 months on 31st July. And for admission in class 1, the child should be 5 years and 6 months on 31st July.
  • The child's birth certificate must be issued by the authority competent to register birth viz. The Municipality Notified Area Committee at the place of birth.
  • For re-admission to std.1, 20th February will be the last day, every year.
  • Admission forms once issued will not be taken back.
  • Address proof (Either latest Telephone Bill, latest Electricity Bill, Maintenance Bill, Aadhar Card, Passport)
  • Office Timing: 8:30AM to 1:30 PM, and 2:00 PM to 3:30 PM (Monday to Saturday)
    •

Documents Required

  • Current School Transcript.
  • 4 Passport size photo of student.
  • Original letter of recommendation from the principal/facilitator of the current school.
  • School leaving certificate (original), Birth certificate (original)
