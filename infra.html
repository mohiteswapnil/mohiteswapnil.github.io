  • Campus

    Spread over a sprawling area, the school campus is designed to cater to the needs of young and growing children. The school building is well-furnished, interiorly decorated & ventilated, highly colourful classrooms for the students of this section give a winsome view. Our school play area is enriched with a variety of toys promising to keep the champs refreshed and chearful! Our school empowers the little ones with landscaped, lushgreen, vibrant playground to harden their muscles and strengten their minds. It provides a healthy and attractive environment and an appropriate theme to make children enjoy every moment of their presence in school.

  • Library

    The school has a spacious, well-stocked library with rich qualitative collections of books and CDs. This serves as a vast knowledge resource for teachers and learners of all ages. Library is the heart of any good educational institute. Little Champ has different category of books including reference books, classics, tales, mysteries, mythology and syllabus related books. Students attend compulsory library class within their academic routine. Teachers guide them and improve their reading habits.



  • Laboratory

    Computer knowledge or skill is no more a hobby. It has now become a necessity for every child in today's world. Our school has a beautiful interiorly decorated well-versed computer lab with more than 20 computers. It is easily accessible for the students. All computers are networked making information available to every student from any terminal. The students are taught not only computers as a subject but also to use it as a window to the outer global world.
