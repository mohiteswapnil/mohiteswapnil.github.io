Academics

Our multidimensional academic programs free students from conventional boundaries, allowing them to learn deeply from many perspectives.

They can explore courses across disciplines and become immersed in multiple areas of learning related to their areas of interest.

At Siddheshwar High School, students will grow intellectually and creatively, expand their problem-solving capabilities, and become prepared to affect a complex world.

  • The Curriculum

    At Siddheshwar High School, the curriculum has been so framed, that the individual child is the core of the whole programme. The students are encouraged to get involved in the exciting learning experience through discussion, enquiry and experimentation.

  • The Academic Year

    The academic year, during which the children are in attendance at the school, extends normally, from June to March. The School is closed for the vacation twice a year for 1 week Christmas Vacation in December and for 5/6 weeks in April/May for Summer Vacation.

  • Examinations & Courses of study

    The school prepares students for the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) Examination of the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education. Graded courses of study, spread over a period of thirteen years, are prescribed, from the Kindergarten to the Higher Secondary Classes. The Medium of instruction is English.

  • Method Of Evaluation

    Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation is done throughout the year as per Central Board norms. Grades are awarded. Students are graded in everything they do. For Std. X there would be Board Examination takes place at the end of the academic year.
