We at Siddheshwar High School, believe in a philosophy, in which the teachers are caretakers & mentors, committed to discover the talents and to provide the best educational environment for each of our students by blending culture, spirituality & modern technology.

We follow child centric method of imparting learning laying emphasis on nurturing a child beyond scholastic level. The school aims at providing a blend of both modern and ancient strategies of teaching ensuring that students develop as confident, intellectual, talented and eloquent citizens of tomorrow.

Siddheshwar High School was established with an aim to teach and nurture the students to develop their creativity, critical thinking and give them a proper channel to develop their personality.

To encourage the students continually to strive after excellence in every field, without however neglecting the welfare and need of their fellow citizens.

We guide our student from every caste, creed and community and strive to educate them in the high ideals of humanism and unselfish service of the citizens of the country.

About Siddheshwar High School


Prof. Sangameshwar Bhairgond
[Founder President]

  • Established in 1987 by Prof. Sangameshwar Bhairgond, with a philosophy of imparting quality education to students, Siddheshwar High School has been focused on creating new benchmarks in the field of education. It is focused on helping students realise their goals and their true potential.

    Prof. Bhairgond believes that every student is a reservoir of tremendous energy which needs to be chanelised and given the right direction. This can be done by making students realise their hidden potentials and by making them realise the importance of their cherished goals.

    Our scholastic record, innovative learning methods and strong commitment to delivering quality through integrity has helped our students scale heights and set a trend of being among the best in all spheres.

    We welcome you to join our family, as Siddheshwar High School continues to be a school that not only teaches, but inspires.
